About Me

I am a Part-Time Lecturer at Northeastern University Vancouver, where I teach business analytics, SQL, database design, statistics, R, and digital transformation.

I hold a Ph.D. in Civil Engineering (Geotechnical and GeoEnvironmental Engineering) from Queen's University. My research interests include environmental sustainability, geoenvironmental materials, air quality modeling, and the application of machine learning and AI in environmental and business analytics.
